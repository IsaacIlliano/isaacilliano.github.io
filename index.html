About me

My name is Isaac, I have a strong interest for network and information security, cybersecurity and data privacy, this passion led me to graduate in Computer and Telecommunication Engineering at Unicas University of Cassino, Italy.
During my Bachelor, I had the opportunity to participate in the Erasmus+ program. Thanks to the Erasmus+ program, I studied for 6 months in Eindhoven (Netherlands). During my period abroad, I studied many IT security issues, which convinced me even more to continue my studies towards that specialization.
My desire to stay up to date on IT and security matters has allowed me to join great communities so I don't miss any news.
Currently I am completing my Master's Degree in Cybersecurity at Sapienza University of Rome, Italy, in order to acquire more knowledge in this field. I am dynamic, correct and determined. and I also define myself as a very ambitious guy.
